#### Short Term Let Consultation ahead of Commonwealth Games 2026

The Licensing and Regulatory Committee at its meeting on 27 August 2025 considered a report by the Director of Legal and Administration in relation to amending its Short Term Lets Policy,
solely to permit a temporary exemption from the licensing regime of Short Term Lets in respect of Glasgow’s hosting of the XXII Commonwealth Games in 2026.
Glasgow’s second time hosting of these Games.

You can read a sample exemption and take the survey here  https://www.smartsurvey.co.uk/s/STLCWG26/
